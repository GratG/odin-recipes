Spinach Pasta

Spinach pasta with Cream sauce

This basic spinach pasta has a cream sauce base, with mushrooms and bacon.

Ingredients:

  • 250g Pasta of your choice
  • 200g Spinach
  • 150g Mushrooms
  • 150g Bacon
  • 30g butter
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 2 tbs of flour

Steps:

  1. boil your spinach for 5 min and strain all water
  2. cook your bacon and set aside
  3. saute the mushrooms
  4. With the mushrooms still in the pan, add all the milk with flour mixed in
  5. with the stove set to low let the sauce thicken and then add butter and seasoning
  6. add bacon and strained spinach to the sauce and mix
  7. finally add the sauce to the cooked pasta
