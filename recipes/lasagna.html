Lasagna

A classic Lasagna with mince and layered pasta, topped with cheese.

Ingredients:

  • 500g minced beef
  • 1/2 diced onion
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 50g tomato paste
  • 1 can of tomatoes
  • lasagna sheets
  • grated mozzarella cheese

Steps:

  1. cook minced beef till light brown
  2. then add diced onion and garlic to the mince
  3. once onion and garlic have sauted then add tomato can and paste, cook for 10min
  4. layer cooked lasagna and mince to a dish
  5. put dish into preheated oven at 180 celcius for 20min(last 5min add cheese)
